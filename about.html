About Heritage Bank

Our Story

Heritage Bank was founded in 1999 with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable banking services that help individuals and families build lasting financial legacies. What started as a single branch in New York City has grown into a trusted institution serving customers across the United States.

For over 25 years, we have remained committed to the principles that guided our founding — transparency, integrity, and putting our customers first. We believe that banking should be straightforward, accessible, and designed to help you achieve your financial goals.

As a Member FDIC institution and Equal Housing Lender, Heritage Bank continues to uphold the highest standards of financial safety and regulatory compliance while embracing modern technology to deliver the banking experience our customers deserve.

Our Journey

1999

Heritage Bank Founded

Opened our first branch in New York's Financial District with a team of 12 dedicated bankers.

2004

Expanded Services

Launched our investment advisory and small business lending programs, reaching 10,000 customers.

2010

Digital Banking Launch

Introduced online banking and mobile apps, making banking accessible 24/7 from anywhere.

2016

Community Milestone

Surpassed $1 billion in community loans and launched the Heritage Community Foundation.

2020

Innovation & Growth

Launched contactless payments, mobile check deposit, and virtual debit cards during a pivotal year for digital banking.

2024

25 Years of Trust

Celebrated 25 years of service with over 500,000 active accounts and branches across the country.

About pain.001 Format

ISO 20022 Customer Credit Transfer Initiation

The pain.001 format is an international standard for initiating credit transfers. It supports:

  • Bulk wire transfers and ACH payments
  • Payroll processing for multiple employees
  • Vendor and supplier payments
  • Cross-border payment instructions

Your XML file should contain a <CstmrCdtTrfInitn> root element with payment instructions in <CdtTrfTxInf> blocks. Download the sample template for an example.

Passkeys vs Passwords: The Future of Banking Authentication

Heritage Bank now supports passkey login. Here's why this biometric-powered technology is more secure than passwords and how to set it up.

Passwords are the weakest link in online security — they can be phished, guessed, leaked in data breaches, and reused across sites. Passkeys replace passwords entirely with biometric authentication (fingerprint, face scan) tied to your specific device.

When you log in with a passkey, your phone or computer proves your identity using cryptographic keys that never leave your device. There's nothing to type, nothing to remember, and nothing a hacker can steal remotely.

To set up passkeys on your Heritage Bank account: Go to Settings → Security → Enable Passkey. You'll authenticate once with your existing credentials, and from then on, a simple fingerprint or face scan logs you in instantly. Heritage Bank still supports traditional passwords and 2FA as fallback options.

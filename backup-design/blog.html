Protecting Yourself from Financial Fraud in the Digital Age

Cybercriminals are becoming more sophisticated. Learn how to recognize and protect yourself from common financial scams...

Phishing emails, fake banking apps, and social engineering calls are on the rise. Never share your password, PIN, or one-time codes with anyone — Heritage Bank will never ask for these via email or phone. Always verify the sender before clicking links, and use unique, strong passwords for every account.

Enable two-factor authentication on your Heritage Bank account, monitor your statements regularly, and set up transaction alerts so you're notified of any activity immediately. If you suspect fraud, contact Heritage Bank's 24/7 fraud hotline right away — early reporting drastically reduces losses.

Understanding Credit Scores: A Complete Guide

Your credit score affects everything from loan rates to job applications. Learn what factors impact your score and how to improve it...

Your credit score is calculated from five main factors: payment history (35%), amounts owed (30%), length of credit history (15%), new credit (10%), and credit mix (10%). The single most impactful thing you can do is pay every bill on time — even one missed payment can drop your score significantly.

Keep credit card balances below 30% of your limit, avoid opening too many new accounts at once, and don't close old cards (they help your average age of credit). Heritage Bank customers can check their credit score for free through their online banking dashboard.

Retirement Planning at Every Age: Your Decade-by-Decade Guide

It's never too early or too late to plan for retirement. Here's what you should be doing in your 20s, 30s, 40s, and beyond...

In your 20s, start contributing to a 401(k) or IRA — even small amounts benefit enormously from compound interest. In your 30s, aim to save at least 15% of income and begin diversifying investments. By your 40s, maximize retirement contributions and pay down high-interest debt.

In your 50s, take advantage of catch-up contributions and start modeling your retirement income needs. Heritage Bank's retirement planning tools and advisors can help you at every stage — whether you're just starting out or fine-tuning your strategy for the final stretch.

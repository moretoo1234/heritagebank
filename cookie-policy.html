1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your experience with Heritage Bank's online services.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which serve comparable purposes. This policy covers all such technologies.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Essential for site functionality, including authentication, security, and session management. These cannot be disabled. Session / up to 24 hours
Performance Help us understand how visitors use our site by collecting anonymous analytics data (page views, error rates). Up to 2 years
Functional Remember your preferences (language, region, display settings) to provide a personalized experience. Up to 1 year
Security Help detect fraud, prevent unauthorized access, and protect your account during online banking sessions. Session / up to 30 days

3. How We Use Cookies

Heritage Bank uses cookies to:

  • Authenticate your identity when you sign in to online banking
  • Maintain your session as you navigate between pages
  • Protect your security by detecting suspicious activity and preventing unauthorized transactions
  • Remember your preferences such as language, accessibility settings, and dashboard layout
  • Analyze site performance to identify and fix errors, optimize loading times, and improve usability
  • Comply with regulations by maintaining audit trails required by banking regulators
Important: Heritage Bank does not use cookies for advertising or marketing purposes. We do not sell cookie data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site may be set by third-party services we use:

  • Font Awesome / Google Fonts: For rendering icons and typography
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): For faster delivery of scripts and stylesheets

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of their cookies. Heritage Bank does not control these cookies but selects partners committed to user privacy.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu. Visit your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cannot be disabled as they are essential for the website to function and for your banking security.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of our online banking services. For example, you may need to sign in more frequently if session cookies are blocked.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through our online banking platform.

About This Statement

Heritage Bank is committed to ensuring that our digital services are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. This statement outlines our accessibility standards, features, and ongoing efforts.

1. Our Commitment

Heritage Bank is committed to ensuring that our website and digital banking services are accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and continually work to improve the digital experience for all users.

We believe that banking should be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. Our accessibility efforts are guided by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act, and international accessibility best practices.

Regulatory Compliance: Heritage Bank complies with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act, and applicable state accessibility laws. We are committed to providing equal access to all of our banking services.

2. Accessibility Features

Our website and online banking platform include the following accessibility features:

Keyboard Navigation: Full site navigation available via keyboard for users who cannot use a mouse.
Screen Reader Support: Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, and alt text for images to support screen readers.
Color Contrast: Text and interactive elements meet WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements.
Resizable Text: Content can be resized up to 200% without loss of functionality or readability.
Form Labels: All form fields include descriptive labels and error messages for assisted input.
Responsive Design: The site adapts to all screen sizes and orientations, including mobile devices.

3. Conformance Status

Heritage Bank's website aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA. We regularly audit our site and correct identified issues. While we strive for full conformance, some areas may be undergoing remediation as we continuously improve our accessibility.

WCAG 2.1 Level AA target conformance
Regular third-party accessibility audits
Automated and manual testing processes
Continuous remediation of identified issues

5. Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

JAWS (Job Access With Speech) for Windows
NVDA (NonVisual Desktop Access) for Windows
VoiceOver for macOS and iOS
TalkBack for Android
Dragon NaturallySpeaking voice recognition
Standard screen magnification tools

6. Ongoing Efforts

Heritage Bank is dedicated to maintaining and improving accessibility. Our ongoing efforts include:

Regular accessibility audits of our website and mobile applications
Staff training on accessibility awareness and inclusive customer service
Incorporating accessibility requirements into our development process
Testing with assistive technologies as part of our quality assurance
Engaging with disability advocacy organizations for feedback
Annual compliance reviews with third-party auditors

7. Feedback & Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Heritage Bank's digital services. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:
